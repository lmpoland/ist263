Where to Donate

I recommend finding local organizations that accept handmade donations of cold-weather clothing and accessories, especially if you live in a colder climate. Churches, community centers, homeless shelters, women’s shelters are great places to donate to and benefit people in your community. Other national organizations such as Project Linus take blankets. Why stop at hats and blankets when you can make stuffed animals, gloves, sweaters and other heartwarming items?
