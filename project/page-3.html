Where to Donate

I recommend finding local organizations that accept handmade donations of cold-weather clothing and accessories, especially if you live in a colder climate. Look within your community to see where help is needed.

Where to look locally:

  • Churches
  • Community centers
  • Homeless shelters
  • Women's shelters
  • Refugee centers
  • Schools

Where to look nationally:

  • Project Linus accpets blankets for children in need
  • Knots of Love accepts hats and blankets for cancer patients
  • Warm up America accepts accessories and 7" x 9" crochet sections
  • The Handmade with Love Project at Operation Gratitude accepts hats and scarves for veterans and troops
